SANCTUARY
Unlike other organizations, the VOSD Sanctuary is no shelter - itss a home. Taking in dogs that are in no condition to return to where they came from, the VOSD dogs are here for life. From medication to nutritious meals twice a day to plenty of space to frolic about and water bodies all around, the VOSD level of TLC just cannot be beaten!

At the moment, we have approximately 600 dogs (and 3 horses!) living with us, and 1 or 2 more coming in each day. Even with hundreds of dogs, however, the VOSD process is a systematic one. Dogs are separated into enclosures based on their temperament. Each enclosure has plenty of room, a regular water supply, floodlights, and heaters to stay warm through the cold.

VOSD never says no to any dog in need, and - true to our mantra - we are currently expanding our facilities to accomodate 1000 dogs.
A GLIMPSE OF HEAVEN
The safe haven for hundreds of happy dogs, the VOSD Sanctuary is truly an enchanted place. With plenty of wagging tails and happy licks, there is no question - VOSD makes dogs happy for life!
